SERVECES OFFERD

Camping Facilities: We provide spacious and well-maintained camping spots where you can pitch your tent and enjoy a comfortable stay amidst nature. Our sites offer privacy and scenic views, allowing you to immerse yourself in the beauty of the surroundings. Outdoor Activities: Explore the wonders of the great outdoors with our diverse range of activities. From hiking and trekking through picturesque trails to fishing in the nearby lakes, there is something for every adventurer. Engage in water sports like kayaking, canoeing, or paddleboarding, or challenge yourself with mountain biking on thrilling trails. Guided Tours: Discover the hidden gems of the region with our expert-guided tours. Our knowledgeable guides will take you on unforgettable journeys, unveiling the secrets of the local flora, fauna, and landmarks. Whether it's a nature walk or a wildlife safari, our tours offer unique insights into the natural wonders that surround us. Campfire Gatherings: Gather around the campfire and indulge in the warmth and camaraderie it brings. Share stories, laughter, and deliciously toasted marshmallows while connecting with fellow campers. Our campfire gatherings create a sense of community and provide a space for cherished memories to be made. Recreational Facilities: Enjoy our recreational facilities designed to enhance your camping experience. Engage in friendly competitions with volleyball or badminton matches, or simply unwind and relax in designated areas for leisure activities. We aim to provide a variety of options for guests of all ages and interests. Amenities: We prioritize your comfort during your stay. Our campsite offers fresh water stations, clean restrooms, and hot showers conveniently located within the premises. We strive to provide a hassle-free experience, ensuring that your basic needs are met. Environmental Stewardship: At Amaya Tamaro, we are committed to sustainable practices. We promote responsible camping, waste management, and eco-friendly initiatives. By minimizing our ecological footprint, we aim to preserve the natural beauty of our surroundings and promote a harmonious coexistence with nature. Special Events and Retreats: We cater to special events and retreats, providing a unique venue for gatherings, team-building activities, and workshops. Whether it's a corporate retreat or a family reunion, we offer customizable packages to suit your specific needs. At Amaya Tamaro, we strive to provide a holistic outdoor experience that combines adventure, relaxation, and a deep connection with nature. Our services are designed to ensure that you have a memorable and rejuvenating stay, creating moments that will stay with you long after you leave our campsite.
